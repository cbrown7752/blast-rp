About Blast RP

Blast RP is new. We are committed to being a safe non-toxic community for 5M roleplay. As we are small, we currently only role play with inside the Blaine county, Sandy shores, grape seed, and Harmony areas. We hope one day to expand and be able to roleplay statewide.

Application

There is no need to apply to be a civilian. Anyone that meets the age requirement can be a civilian. All the other applications are as follows:

BCSO - https://blastrp.tk/BCSO 

Fire - https://blastrp.tk/Fire 

Dispatch - https://blastrp.tk/Dispatch  


Our age limit

This community adheres to an age limit to help maintain our goals success. Everyone ages 13+ are allowed to submit an application/become a CIV.
